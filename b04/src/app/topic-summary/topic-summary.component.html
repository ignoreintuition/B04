Topic Summary

UNCC is the Charlotte campus for University of North Carolina. This site serves as a portal to find all the latest news, featured stories, events, media, and student activities that the campus offers. Up-to-date news is taken from Inside UNC Charlotte which is maintained by the Office of University Communications. Faculty and staff members submit their articles to be published to bring students all the lastest information. The site is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, allowing students to quickly find the information they need.

UNCC dates back to the 1940s when it was founded as Charlotte College and grown to be the third largest enrollement in the UNC system. UNCC provides eight different Academic Colleges (Art & Architecture, Business, Computing, Education, Engineering, Health & Human Services, Science, and Humanities) as well as the Honors College and Academic College.
